About Me

My Story

I am a UX Designer who recently completed a 400+ hour UX Immersion course at Careerfoundry. Prior to becoming a UX Designer, I graduated with a BBA from Temple University, and spent 6 years as a Project Manager within the construction and manufacturing industries.

In addition to the UX course, I am currently completing a Front End Development course. My goal with this course is to make myself a well rounded UX Designer capable of efficiently communicating with development teams.

I love the outdoors and traveling without an itinerary. Proud (unofficial) member of the All-50 club.
